About Me

Hello and welcome! I am a graduate mechanical engineer in the DFW area. My interests are in control theory, dynamic modeling, and simulation for complex dynamical systems. Feel free to look into my project portfolio, or get in contact with me!

I am currently completing my Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering at The University of Texas at Dallas, where I have built a strong foundation in linear systems, optimal control, robust control, model predictive control, dynamic programming, vehicle dynamics, and engineering optimization.

My technical work combines modeling, simulation, controls, and practical engineering implementation. I enjoy building systems that connect theory with realistic constraints, whether that means nonlinear quadcopter control in Simulink, vehicle control under friction limits, turbine performance analytics, or mechanical design for manufacturing environments.

Technical Interests

  • Dynamic modeling and simulation
  • Model predictive control
  • Vehicle dynamics and control
  • Nonlinear systems
  • Renewable energy systems
